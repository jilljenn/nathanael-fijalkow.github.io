Why do I no longer submit in conferences?



Usual arguments for submitting to (prestigious) conferences
    I want my results to be visible and quickly accessible (journal submissions take too long)
    I want to be informed about the latest important results
    I want my students to thrive and get positions
    I do not want to be scientifically isolated
