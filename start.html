Hello! I am a computer scientist. My research interests include games, automata, and logics, program verification, learning theory, process algebra, and dynamical systems. In all of these topics I focus on stochastic systems.



I am a research fellow of the Alan Turing Institute of data science in London, affiliated with University of Warwick, as well as honorary senior research associate in the PPLV group at University College London (UCL).

David Pym and I are leading the Logics for Data Science group at the Turing.
